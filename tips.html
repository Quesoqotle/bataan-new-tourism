Travel Tips

☀️
Best Time to Visit

The dry season from November to May is ideal for beach trips and hiking. March and April are especially significant for historical commemorations.

🚌
Getting Around

Buses from Manila (Genesis or Bataan First) go directly to Balanga or Mariveles. Locally, tricycles and jeepneys are the primary modes of transport.

🛡️
Safety Reminders

Stay hydrated when hiking Mt. Samat. When visiting beaches, follow local lifeguard warnings and be mindful of current changes.

💳
Money & ATM

Major towns like Balanga have plenty of ATMs, but carry enough cash when heading to remote areas or small beach resorts.

🤝
Respect & Etiquette

Always show respect at war memorials and shrines. It is customary to ask permission before taking photos of locals or private property.

😁
Have Fun

You are here to have fun and enjoy the breathtaking scenery or the mouth-watering gastronomy.
