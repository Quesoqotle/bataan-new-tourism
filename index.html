Experience Bataan

Escape the city rush and rediscover calm.

Bataan is a peninsula in Central Luzon that blends scenic coastlines with rich history. From the historic Bataan Death March to modern eco-tourism, it offers a refreshing getaway just hours from Manila.

Why visit? Whether you're a history buff, a nature lover, or someone looking for a quiet beach retreat, Bataan has a place for you.
